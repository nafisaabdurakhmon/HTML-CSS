General information

The OFS company has been sewing men's suits in Uzbekistan for 20 years, being one of the founders of a new direction in clothing sewing in the country.

Our specialists regularly visit exhibitions on this type of goods, study the market and fashion trends, and are constantly searching for new youth models. Using modern technologies in the clothing industry, OFS has become one of the largest companies that produces a full range of high-quality men's classic clothing

Business, wedding, classic and youth models of men's trouser suits, jackets, trousers, children's trouser suits and women's suits.

In the large design hall of the OFS fashion house in a pleasant atmosphere in the very center of Bukhara you can choose and order suits made according to your individual measurements.

Our brands “OFS”, “ARMADA”, “FORVARD”, “STATUS”, “CARLTON”, “ALBILIO”, “EGIGAST”, “DIE HARD”, “GERAR”, “VIATTI”

Guarantee comfort during wearing and high-quality appearance of products, which is preserved for a long time. "OFS" has a large customer base, which is due to the optimal price-quality ratio.

Our products are bought both in Uzbekistan and in neighboring foreign countries.

We offer wholesale buyers regularly updated collections, selecting the most interesting materials. The OFS company always values ​​the trust of its potential partners.
